Resume

Infinite Experiment is a passion project I work on during my spare time. It’s a hypercasual one-button infinite runner inspired by the likes of Flappy Bird. You control a rotating ship and move forward in the direction the ship is facing when you tap the screen, while dodging obstacles along the way.

My goal with this game is to be a part of a project from beginning to end, and working on a project like this lets me get experience in all aspects of game development - it’s a perfect way to practice my coding skills, implement art and sound assets, and learn how to create games for mobile.
