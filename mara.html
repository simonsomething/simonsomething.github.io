Mara was a university project with students from different disciplines. It's a nightmarish Soulslike with a health system that employs buffs and debuff rather than a simple health bar. I spent most of my time creating levels and brainstorming ideas for this health system. I wanted to create eerie experiences to go along with the theme of the game, and to achieve this I explored level design concepts like spatial empathy, as well as researched how emotions are developed through architecture. I was also in charge of writing the game design document.

The greatest challenge was creating mechanical penalties that didn't ruin the "fun" of the game, but through some long brainstorming sessions, playtesting and identifying problems we came up with a healthy mix of debuffs for the players to enjoy.

Artwork and assets by Elin Öhrman, Ludvig Prütz, Simon Sääf Malm and Sofie Norgren. Sketches by me.
